Public Schools Athletic League

MOBILE APP

New York City has the largest public school athletic system in the country (PSAL). Over 150,000 people use its website every month; mostly from mobile devices.

We created a mobile app to help athletes, staff, and fans follow their favorite sports on the go. I was the UX lead on the project, while supporting our dev team and project management

Research
& Interviews

Google Analytics revealed clear trends in user behavior. I could see that traffic consistently spiked after school and in the evenings after events.

But Why?

To learn more, my team interviewed athletes, coaches, parents, PSAL staff, and athletic directors.

We learned that people rely on the website to find event locations, rosters, rules and regulations. However, the site wasn’t designed for mobile devices, so people ended up calling PSAL’s office for help

Specs & User Flows

I wrote all of the functional specs for our dev team and worked with PSAL to design their web services.

I also used Omnigraffle to create an interactive map of user flows. This helped our stakeholders understand the nuances of the app and visualize the progress we were making.

Design
& Development

The app’s design strives to capture our users’ passion for sports; without sacrificing usability. That’s why we made sure to curate its content to each user needs.

Everyone from officials to parents and even college scouts will find this app to be a simple and indispensable tool. The iPhone app will be released this summer, with an Android version following next year.
